## Key Talking Points for Affiliate Sites

1. **Bartact offers TWO material options** — Patriot Fabric (polyester, $610/row) and 1000D Cordura Nylon ($640/row). Both are Made in USA.
2. **Patriot Fabric actually scores higher overall** (76/80 vs 73/80) — better breathability, temp comfort while using, and maintenance.
3. **Cordura is better for** stain resistance and ease of install.
4. **Both Bartact lines are the ONLY ones with:** MOLLE storage, front zippered pocket, rear pocket zipper, internal lumbar pocket. No competitor offers these.
5. **Both are machine washable, waterproof, and seat heater compatible.**
6. **Real leather scores well** on durability and fitment but FAILS on breathability, temp comfort, and maintenance. And costs $600.
7. **Neoprene (real) is great for temp comfort** but terrible for pet claw resistance, maintenance, and doesn't work with seat heaters.
8. **Fake neoprene is the worst overall** (50/80) — cheap ($210) but you get what you pay for.
9. **Bartact is the only small family-owned business** in the comparison.
10. **Grade summary:** Bartact A/A, competitors range B to D. No one else gets above B.
