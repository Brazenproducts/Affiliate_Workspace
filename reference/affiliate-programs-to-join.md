**Approval:** Manual review, typically 3–7 business days  

**Required Fields:**
- Publisher name (use: Brazen Products / BestTruckAccessories.com)
- Primary website URL: `https://besttruckaccessories.com`
- Email: `info@brazenauto.com`
- Traffic source description (review/comparison site — be specific)
- Primary promotional method (content marketing, product reviews)
- EIN/Tax ID for W-9 (required for payout)
- PayPal or bank account for payments

**Notes:**
- Once CJ account is active, apply individually to each brand program within CJ
- besttruckaccessories.com is ideal — it's a review site with focused truck/auto content, many pages, and it already links Amazon products with `brazenprodu01-20` tag
- When applying to sub-programs (AutoZone, Advance Auto, Cabela's), mention truck/Jeep/off-road audience specifically
- Minimum payout: $50

**Best Sites to List:**
- Primary: `besttruckaccessories.com` (automotive review, most relevant)
- Secondary: `brazenauto.com` (store — shows commercial credibility)

### 3. Walmart Affiliates (Own Platform)
**Signup URL:** https://affiliates.walmart.com  
**Commission:** ~1–4% (varies by category; automotive/tools typically 1–2%)  
**Cookie:** 3-day  
**Approval:** Very fast — often approved within 1 business day  
**Auto-approve eligible:** Near-auto-approve for established content sites  

**Required Fields:**
- Name and email
- Website URL: `https://besttruckaccessories.com`
- Social media (optional)
- Must be 18+ and US-based
- Tax form (W-9 for US publishers)

**Notes:**
- Walmart has its **own affiliate platform** (NOT through Impact as previously thought)
- Link generator available immediately after approval at affiliates.walmart.com
- Product search and storefront tools available in the Member Center
- Weekly newsletter with banners and product links
- Great for items sold at Walmart: floor mats, seat covers, tools, filters
- Minimum payout: $10

**Why apply here first:** Fastest approval, and Walmart carries many auto accessories. Can add links to site almost immediately.
