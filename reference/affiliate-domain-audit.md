## Key Takeaways

1. **Highest immediate potential:** `tacticalseats.com`, `tacomaseats.com`, `tacticalseatcovers.com` — high-ticket automotive/tactical crossover with strong search volume and EMD advantage.

2. **Best volume play:** `bestwindshieldwiper.com`, `homehvacfilters.com`, `whatarebest.com` — massive search volume keywords, though lower per-sale revenue.

3. **Sleeper picks:** `bedtread.com` (great brandable name + high ticket), `bigrigseatcovers.com` (underserved niche), `petwearhouse.com` (huge market + clever domain).

4. **Cluster opportunities:** Several domains group into mini-networks:
   - **HVAC filters:** `homehvacfilters.com`, `besthvacfilter.com`, `besthomefilter.com`, `filtersubscriptions.com`, `hvacprefilter.com`, `furnaceprefilter.com`
   - **Welding safety:** `weldgloves.com`, `weldingsleeves.com`, `weldaprons.com`
   - **Sandpaper/abrasives:** `sandpapergrit.com`, `drumsanderpaper.com`, `palmsanderpaper.com`, `bestorbitalsandpaper.com`
   - **Fire safety:** `kitchenfireextinguisher.com`, `autoextinguishers.com`, `extinguishersales.com`
   - **Tactical/MOLLE:** `tacticalseats.com`, `tacticalseatcovers.com`, `molleattachments.com`, `tacticalpatchesusa.com`

5. **Revenue model note:** These estimates assume a single-author content site ranking on page 1 for primary keywords within 6–12 months. Actual revenue depends heavily on content quality, backlink profile, and Amazon conversion rates. The HVAC filter cluster has the best recurring revenue potential due to replacement cycles.
