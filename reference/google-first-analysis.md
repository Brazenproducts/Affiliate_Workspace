# Google-First vs Amazon-First Analysis
# Which domains target categories where people search Google BEFORE Amazon?

## Google-First Categories (People Research First)
These are products where buyers:
- Compare options/brands before buying
- Need fitment/compatibility info
- Spend $100+ (higher consideration)
- Want reviews/guides before committing
- Search "[product] for [vehicle]" or "best [product] 2026"

## Amazon-First Categories (People Go Straight to Amazon)
- Commodity/consumable items
- Under $30, low consideration
- Brand doesn't matter much
- "I know exactly what I need"
