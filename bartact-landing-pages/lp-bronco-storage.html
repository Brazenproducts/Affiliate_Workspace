FAQ

Common questions from owners.

Do these MOLLE panels fit both the 2-Door and 4-Door Bronco?
Bartact makes separate MOLLE panel fitments for the 2-Door and 4-Door Bronco. Make sure you select the correct configuration when ordering. The cargo liner is also specific to the 4-Door model due to the different cargo area dimensions.
Will these work with the Bronco Raptor?
Most Bartact Bronco accessories are compatible with the Bronco Raptor, but always verify fitment in the product description or contact Bartact directly. The Raptor has some interior differences that may affect specific products.
Do I need to drill to install the MOLLE panels?
No drilling required. Bartact's MOLLE panels are designed to use existing mounting points in the Bronco's interior. Installation is straightforward and reversible — no permanent modifications to your vehicle.
Are the cargo pouches compatible with other MOLLE systems?
Yes — Bartact's cargo pouches use standard MOLLE/PALS webbing and are compatible with any MOLLE-compatible panel or vest. They work great on Bartact panels as well as military surplus gear, backpacks, and other MOLLE-equipped accessories.
