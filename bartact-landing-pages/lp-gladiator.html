FAQ

Common questions from owners.

Do these fit the Gladiator Mojave and 392 editions?
Yes — but you need the specific Mojave/392 edition covers, not the standard Gladiator covers. The Mojave and 392 have different seat shapes. Bartact makes dedicated patterns for both. Check the product description carefully or contact Bartact to confirm fitment for your trim.
Are these compatible with the side airbags on my Gladiator?
Yes — Bartact offers SRS airbag-compliant versions of their Tactical seat covers. These are specifically designed to allow the side airbags to deploy correctly in the event of a collision. Always select the SRS-compliant option if your Gladiator is equipped with side airbags.
Will these work with the rear fold-down armrest on my Gladiator?
The rear bench covers come in two versions: with and without the fold-down armrest. Make sure you select the correct version for your specific Gladiator configuration when ordering.
What's the difference between Tactical and Base Line Performance covers?
Base Line Performance covers use heavy-duty canvas and deliver a clean, rugged look with excellent durability. Tactical covers add MOLLE/PALS webbing on the seat backs so you can mount pouches, tools, and gear directly to your seats. Both are made in the USA — it comes down to whether you want the MOLLE functionality.
