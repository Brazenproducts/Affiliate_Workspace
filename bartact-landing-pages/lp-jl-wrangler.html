JL/JLU FAQ

Common questions from JL and JLU owners.

Will these fit my 2018+ JL 2-Door AND the JLU 4-Door?
They're different products — Bartact makes separate patterns for the JL 2-Door and the JLU 4-Door to ensure a perfect fit on each. Make sure you select the correct model when ordering. The JLU rear bench also comes in two versions: with and without the fold-down armrest.
Are these compatible with the side-curtain airbags on my JL?
Yes — Bartact offers SRS airbag-compliant versions of their Tactical seat covers. These are specifically designed to allow the side airbags to deploy correctly in the event of a collision. Always select the SRS-compliant option if your JL is equipped with side airbags (most 2018+ models are).
Do these work with the Mojave or 392 Edition JLU?
The standard JL/JLU covers are NOT for Mojave or 392 Edition models — those seats have a different shape. Bartact makes dedicated covers for the Mojave and 392 editions. Check the product description carefully or contact Bartact to confirm fitment for your specific trim.
What's the difference between Base Line Performance and Tactical?
Base Line Performance covers ($299.99) use heavy-duty canvas and deliver a clean, rugged look with excellent durability. Tactical covers ($469–$490) add MOLLE/PALS webbing on the seat backs so you can mount pouches, tools, and gear directly to your seats. Both are made in the USA and fit identically — it comes down to whether you want the MOLLE functionality.
