## SkipATip Business Model — FINALIZED (5/12)
- **TAM**: ~250,000 tip-free restaurants in the US (25% of ~1M total)
- **Revenue at full penetration**: 250,000 × $25/month = $6.25M/month = $75M/year
- **Two-tier listing model**:
  - FREE / Unverified: ⚠️ badge, buried below certified, no featured placement. Hook to get restaurants in.
  - CERTIFIED: $49 one-time verification + $25/month recurring. ✅ badge, top of results, featured on city pages, drives foot traffic.
- **Why restaurants pay**: Free listing is invisible. Certified listing gets customers. $25/month pays for itself with one new table.
- **Chain locations**: Listed free/unverified (publicly verifiable, no fraud risk, gives volume)
- **Independent restaurants**: Must self-submit and verify. Unverified badge until certified.
- **NO discount/payment model**: Rejected — too complex, restaurants have better deals on own apps
- **NO fast-casual chains with tip screens**: Chipotle, Sweetgreen, Shake Shack, Panera EXCLUDED
- **Acquisition target**: GrubHub, Yelp, OpenTable, DoorDash — at 500K MAU + 5K paying restaurants, $5-20M acquisition range
