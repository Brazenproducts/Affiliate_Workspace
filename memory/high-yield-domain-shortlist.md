## 🎯 KEY INSIGHT: Luxury Beauty (10%) is a TRAP

All 13 luxury beauty queries had **ZERO Amazon presence in the top 10**. Top results dominated by Allure, Bluemercury, Neiman Marcus, Sephora, Dermstore, etc. **Not affiliate-friendly** — even though commission is 10%, we'd never rank because Amazon doesn't even rank, so users won't be predisposed to click Amazon links from our review.

**Verdict:** Skip luxury beauty for now. Revisit if we figure out a different traffic angle.
